About Me

Hey there! My name is Tyler Gast. I was born August 10th, 1996 making me 28 years old. I grew up near Lake Wissota in Chippewa Falls WI with my mom, dad, and one older brother. Growing up, my brother and I raced motocross almost every weekend from April - October for about 10 years and I still ride to this day. I graduated from Chippewa Falls Senior High school in 2014. After high school I had no real plans of what I wanted to do for a career, but that changed a couple of years ago when I decided to go back to school. Growing up I always felt like I was good with computers and gaming and things like that, so I decided to enroll in the IT - Software Development program offered at Chippewa Valley Technical College. I graduate with honors soon, and I'm excited and eager to start my career in the computer industry.
