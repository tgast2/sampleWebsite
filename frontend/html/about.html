Who Am I?

Hey there! My name is Tyler Gast, and I was born on August 10th, 1996, making me 28 years old. I grew up near Lake Wissota in Chippewa Falls, WI, with my mom, dad, and older brother. My brother and I spent about 10 years racing motocross nearly every weekend from April through October, and I still enjoy riding to this day.

I graduated from Chippewa Falls Senior High School in 2014. At the time, I wasn’t sure what I wanted to do for a career, but that changed a few years ago when I decided to go back to school. I've always felt confident with computers, gaming, and technology, so I enrolled in the IT - Software Development program at Chippewa Valley Technical College.

I’m proud to say I just graduated with honors, and I’m excited and eager to begin my career in the computer industry.
