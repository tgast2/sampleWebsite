My Family

This is us! My little family is made up of my beautiful wife, Jessica, and our two amazing kids: Aspen (5) and Oden (3). Jessica and I started "dating" when we were just 14 years old. We moved into our first apartment in Eau Claire, WI, while she was still a senior in high school (she graduated in 2015, a year after I did). That apartment became the place we came home to after getting married in 2017.

By 2019, we had moved back to Chippewa Falls, WI. In May of that year, we welcomed our daughter Aspen into the world, and 22 short months later, our son Oden joined us. Today, we live in our first house in Fall Creek, WI. Jessica works as a hairstylist at Shear Experience in Eau Claire, Aspen started kindergarten this year, and I can't wait to make more memories together.
