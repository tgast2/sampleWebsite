Our Pets

We love our animals—Nova (our big girl), Arlo (our little boy), and Gracie! Gracie is our 16- year-old cat who mostly keeps to herself, except when it’s time to fill her food bowl. Growing up, my family always had rescue dogs, so I knew when I started my own family that I wanted to continue that tradition.

We rescued both of our dogs from the Clark County Humane Society. Nova joined our family in April 2023 as a tiny puppy, and while she’s grown a bit bigger than we expected, we love her all the same. Arlo came along in March 2024, also as a puppy, but unlike Nova, he has stayed close to his original size.

These two are inseparable—you’ll often find them cuddled on the couch or chasing each other nonstop around the backyard. They bring so much joy and energy to our family!
